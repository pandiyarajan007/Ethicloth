About Us

Ethicloth is dedicated to advancing creative circularity by linking deadstock fabrics from prestigious luxury Maisons with fashion and textile professionals. We offer high-quality materials at affordable prices, with low minimum order requirements and no production delays, empowering designers, talents, and brands to create collections with minimal environmental impact.

Through Ethicloth’s online resale platform, professionals can purchase materials by the roll or panel, based on available stock. Customers can also order fabric samples, arrange worldwide delivery, and access detailed care instructions directly on the platform.
